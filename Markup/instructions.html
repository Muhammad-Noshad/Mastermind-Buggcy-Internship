Mastermind

Controls

  1. Click on any color on the bottom section of the mastermind game board to select that color.
  2. After selecting a color, click on any empty big circle of currently active row to place the selected color there.
  3. Click on the check button to see how close your guess was.

Objective

The objective of Mastermind is to guess the secret code created by the computer within a limited number of attempts. The code is a sequence of colored pegs, and you need to guess the correct colors in the correct order.

How to Play

  1. The computer generates a random secret code at the beginning of the game. The code is a sequence of 4 colors. The colors can't repeat.
  2. You will have a certain number of attempts to guess the secret code. Each guess consists of a sequence of 4 colors.
  3. To make a guess, select a color for each position in the sequence and submit your guess.
  4. After each guess, you will receive feedback in the form of red and white small circles adjacent to the row where you place your guess:
    • Red small circle:
      A red small circle indicates that one of your guessed colors is correct and in the correct position.
    • White small circle:
      A white small circle indicates that one of your guessed colors is correct but in the wrong position.
    • Dark small circle:
      A dark small circle indicates that none of the guessed colors are correct.
  5. Use the feedback to refine your guesses. Repeat the process until you either guess the correct code or run out of attempts.

Winning the Game

You win the game if you correctly guess the secret code within the allowed number of attempts. If you run out of attempts, you lose.

Scoring

You will be given a score based upon the number of guess attempts. The lesser the attempts the better the score.

  • Max obtainable score: 100
  • Min obtainable score: 0

Example

Suppose the secret code is Red, Blue, Green, Yellow.

You guess: Red, Green, Blue, Purple.

The feedback would be:

  • 1 Red small circle (Red is correct and in the correct position).
  • 2 White small circles (Green and Blue are correct colors but in the wrong positions).

Continue making guesses using the feedback until you crack the code or exhaust your attempts. Good luck!
